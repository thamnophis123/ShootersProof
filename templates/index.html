Free target analysis for rifle and rimfire shooters. Upload a photo of your target and get the real statistics that tell you what your groups actually mean.

Free to use · No account required

Step 1

Upload your target

Use any target with a known measurement — 1" grid squares, a center circle, or a printed scale. A clear phone photo works fine.

Step 2

Analyze your shots

Let the computer detect bullet holes automatically, or click each one yourself. Set the scale and get real statistics instantly.

Step 3

Track your progress

Create a free account to save sessions, develop a load, and see how you compare to other shooters by caliber, distance, and rifle.

A note on data: Your target images and shot data are yours. Equipment details you choose to share may appear in anonymized aggregate research comparing loads, rifles, and accessories. We never sell personal information or identify individual users without explicit consent.
